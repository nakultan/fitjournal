SCREEN EIGHT

System-wide

Changes that don't belong to a single screen — visible everywhere.
★ Theme softening · P3.6

Pure #000 retired. Dark theme becomes oklch(0.12 0.01 250) — a hint of cool, more "premium app", less "developer terminal". Light theme paper-2 also softens 4 points toward warm.

→ # 000 → oklch(0.12 0.01 250)
★ Page-header rhythm · P3.7

Hub screens (Today, Progress) get a heavier display title with a monospace ambient strip above. Tool screens (Plan, Recipes, Settings) keep a lighter sub-display. The header tells you the kind of screen you're on, not just its name.

SAT · 12🔥 · 3/4Push Day vs. Plan
★ Save-state dot in every PageHeader · P0.4

The 6px green ripple from P1.1 (already shipped to Today) extends to every screen's PageHeader. Quiet, persistent trust signal. No state means "no banner shown ≠ saved" — explicit signalling.

★ Reorder ▲▼ FLIP animation · P3.8

150ms transition on swap. Tap → result connection is preserved. Used by Today (lifts), Plan (template exercises) and Recipes (ingredient lists).

★ Diverse empty states · P1.5

Each screen gets its own metaphor + copy. No more 6× icon-title-paragraph-button. Examples:

  • Today empty: a chalkboard pattern with "Today's a fresh page."
  • Progress/Story empty: "Train once and a story starts."
  • Records empty: a single-rep dot with "Your first PR is one rep away."
  • History empty: a heatmap of grey squares waiting to fill.
  • Plan empty: a calendar with one circled day.
  • Recipes empty: 3 seeded starters with "We started you off."
★ A11y polish bundle · P1.10

Tab strips gain role="tablist" · date-label gains aria-haspopup="dialog" · arrow-key navigation across segmented controls · skip-link already in place.
